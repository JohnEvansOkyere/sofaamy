# Sofaamy — Information We Need (and why)

*Working checklist for Evans. Each answer unlocks a specific part of the build; the ⭐ items block demo realism the most. Ask for real documents/samples wherever possible — a photo of a paper cutting list teaches more than an hour of description.*

---

## A. Profiles & systems ⭐ (blocks: real cut lists, real BOQs, real prices)

1. Which **profile systems/brands** do you fabricate with? (EvA showed VEKA, ALULINE, SCHUCO, GULF EXTRUSION… — what's Sofaamy's actual list and who supplies them in Accra?)
2. Full **profile list per system**: name/code, what it's used for (frame, mullion, transom, sash, bead, interlock), **price per bar or per metre (GHS)**.
3. Confirm **stock bar lengths**: we saw 5800 mm (mollium), 5750 (transum), 5700 (sash). Same for every system? Do lengths vary by supplier or batch?
4. Is "mollium / transum / sash" the naming everyone in the factory uses? Any other member names we should adopt so screens read natively?

## B. Cutting practice ⭐ (blocks: trustworthy optimizer output)

5. **Kerf** — how many mm does one saw cut consume? (We placeholder 5 mm.)
6. **Minimum reusable offcut** — shortest leftover you keep vs scrap? (We placeholder 300 mm.) Do you store/reuse offcuts today, and would you want the system to track the remnant rack?
7. **Mitre or butt corners** — which members are cut at 45°, which square? (Changes every length in the cut list.)
8. In your optimizer there are per-piece **Grinding (top/left/bottom/right)** and **Rotatable** flags — what do they mean in your process, and do they change length or just machining?
9. **Deduction rules**: when a sash sits in a 1500 mm section, how much smaller is the sash than the section (clearances/overlaps per system)? A sample fabrication drawing answers this instantly.
10. Do you cut **per job or in batches** across jobs? How often? Manual saw or CNC (brand/model + file format if CNC)?
11. What's your current **average waste %**? (Becomes the benchmark we beat in the demo.)

## C. Glass ⭐ (blocks: real glass pricing + glass BOQ)

12. Full **glass list actually stocked/ordered**: type + thickness (e.g. 6 mm clear, 5 mm frosted toughened, 10/12 mm toughened for frameless) with **GHS price per m²**.
13. Who processes/toughens glass for you (in-house or supplier)? What format do they want orders in — sizes list, DXF?
14. Rules of thumb you apply: which products *must* get toughened/laminated (doors, balustrades, canopies)?

## D. Hardware & accessories (blocks: accurate hardware costing)

15. **Hardware sets per opening type** (sliding: rollers+lock+…; casement: hinges+handle+stay; door: hinges+lock+closer) with GHS set prices.
16. Accessories/consumables you cost per job: gaskets, corner cleats, screws, silicone, packers — priced per metre/per unit or as a % allowance?

## E. Finishes (blocks: finish-aware pricing + correct bar grouping)

17. Which **surface finishes** do you offer (powder coat, anodized, wood-finish, PVDF, lamination) and standard colours?
18. How does finish affect **price** — do you buy pre-finished bars per colour, or coat after fabrication (price adder per m² or per bar)?

## F. Products & templates (blocks: the design library matching reality)

19. Your **full product list** with typical/min/max sizes and standard configurations (the Information Requirements doc asked this — chase the answer): windows (sliding/casement/awning/louvre), doors, curtain wall, partitions, balustrades, canopies, frameless (shopfront, shower).
20. Your 10 most common designs — if we pre-build them as templates, the demo shows *their* products, not generic ones.
21. For frameless work: what does SmartGlazier give you today (drawings? DXF? hardware lists?) and what would you need from us to replace or complement it?

## G. Pricing & quoting (blocks: quotes management will sign off)

23. Standard **margin** — one number or varies by product/client? Who's allowed to change it on a quote?
24. Which costs appear on a quote as separate lines vs bundled? (Transport? Installation? Scaffolding?)
25. A copy of a **real recent quotation** (any format) — we'll match the layout so it feels like theirs on day one.
26. When do you need **manual price overrides** (EvA demoed this) — which lines get adjusted most often and why?

## H. Workflow, people & stages (blocks: pipeline tracker + roles)
