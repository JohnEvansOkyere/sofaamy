Section 01
System Overview

The Sofaamy Business Management System is a unified, web-based platform that digitises the full lifecycle of a glass and aluminium job — from the moment a client makes contact, through design and estimation, to factory production and final delivery. The system is built across three tightly integrated modules.

Module 01

Field & Client Intake

Structured digital forms for field reps on-site and walk-in clients. All job data captured in real time. Supervisor notified instantly on submission.

Module 02

Visual Configurator & Estimator

Parametric design canvas where supervisors build live visuals with editable dimensions. Auto-generates client quote PDF, engineer spec sheet, and bill of materials simultaneously.

Module 03

Internal Job Pipeline Tracker

Full job tracking from intake through factory stages to delivery. Role-based views, stage notifications, payment gates, and management analytics dashboard.

Section 05
Job Pipeline — Full State Machine

Every job moves through 13 defined stages from creation to closure. No stage can be skipped. Each transition triggers a notification to the responsible team member. Timestamps are recorded at every stage for performance analytics.

Section 06
Visual Configurator — Parametric Engine Logic

The configurator is the most technically complex component of the system. It uses a parametric constraint engine built on React-Konva. When any dimension changes, the geometry recalculates and the canvas redraws in under 100ms without a page refresh.

Section 07
User Roles & Access Matrix

Every user has a defined role with access restricted to only what their job requires. Access is enforced at both the UI level (menus hidden) and the database level (Row-Level Security policies).

RoleCan ViewCan Create / EditCannot Access
Field Rep Jobs they submitted. Status of submitted jobs. New intake form. Upload photos. Edit own submissions. Designs, quotes, costs, pipeline, factory, payments
Project Supervisor All jobs assigned to them. Full intake details. Designs, visual configurator, quotes. Move jobs to design/quote stages. Payment confirmation. Procurement orders. Factory stages.
Accounts All jobs at "Client Approved" stage. Payment history. Confirm payment. Add payment reference. Unlock job to procurement. Designs, configurator, factory stages, QA.
Procurement Jobs unlocked by Accounts. BOM per job. Mark materials ordered. Update delivery dates. Confirm materials received. Client data, costs, configurator, QA, delivery.
Factory Floor Jobs in their stage. Engineer spec sheet per job. Move job to next factory stage. Add completion notes. Client info, costs, payment, all other stages they don't own.
QA Team Jobs reaching QA. QA checklist per product. Approve for delivery. Flag for rework with reason. Fill QA checklist. All pre-factory stages, payment, procurement.
Management All jobs, all stages, all data. Analytics dashboard. Override any stage. Create user accounts. Update material prices. Nothing — full system access.
