# Frame Projects — Information Request for the Team

## Purpose

We understand that the client-facing quotation is intentionally summarized. The selling price is normally presented as a bundled unit price, while the company still needs a detailed internal breakdown of material cost, labour cost, installation cost, and profit.

The questions below are intended to capture the rules used by the contract, procurement, production, QA, and installation teams so the Frame Projects workflow can be modelled accurately.

Please answer using one completed real project wherever possible. Ideally, provide the client quotation and the corresponding internal costing, cutting list, and production information for the same project.

## 1. Confirmed current workflow

Please confirm whether this sequence is correct:

1. Client provides measurements or Sofaamy sends a representative to site.
2. The representative records the preliminary measurements and site information.
3. The contract manager or supervisor prepares the quotation.
4. The quotation uses bundled rates for each product/system, while internal costs are broken down separately.
5. Similar-project images may be sent to the client for reference.
6. The client approves the quotation and makes the agreed payment, commonly 70–80% upfront.
7. A final site measurement may be taken before production.
8. Procurement receives the approved costing, materials requirements, and accurate cutting list.
9. Production cuts and fabricates according to the approved cutting list.
10. QA checks the work during production and confirms that it is acceptable.
11. Installation is scheduled after production and QA are complete.
12. The project is installed and closed out.

Please identify any missing approval, payment, inspection, or handover stage.
